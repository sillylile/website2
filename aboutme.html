Hi, I'm Lily!

I'm a 1st year Literature, Media, and Communications major at the Georgia Institute of Technology from Atlanta, Georgia. For a lot of my life, I've been fascinated by the different mediums that make up media as a whole, particularly the creation of online content. I don't know exactly what I want to do with this degree yet, but I know I want to be in some sort of digital design job one day.

On campus, I'm involved with my sorority, I serve on the iGniTe Summer Program Student Advisory Board, and I'm a student worker for a digital workshop for the Ivan Allen College of Liberal Arts. I have experience with Java, HTML, and CSS coding languages, I enjoy problem-solving activites, and I love working with others. When I'm not in class or studying, I like to go to the movies, explore new places (particularly on campus!), or actively enjoying the company of my friends.
